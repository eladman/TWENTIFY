The Science

If we can't cite it,
it's not in your plan.

No bro-science. No influencer opinions. No trends. Peer-reviewed research or it doesn't make the cut.

🏋️

Compound > Isolation

Multi-joint movements activate 85% more total muscle fiber than isolation exercises. Fewer exercises, more results per minute.

Schoenfeld et al., 2017
📊

Progressive Overload

Gradually increasing load over time beats changing exercises constantly. The single most evidence-backed driver of strength gains.

Kraemer & Ratamess, 2004
🏃

Zone 2 Is the 20% of Cardio

80% of elite endurance training happens at low intensity. Zone 2 running builds mitochondrial density more effectively than HIIT for most people.

Seiler, 2010
🍽

Protein Timing Is Overrated

Total daily protein intake matters far more than meal timing or supplement windows. Hit the number, hit it consistently. The rest is noise.

Schoenfeld & Aragon, 2018
