App Store Connect — Privacy Disclosure

Data Linked to You:
  - Contact Info (Email Address) — used for account creation and authentication
  - Health & Fitness (Workout data) — used to provide personalized workout plans and track progress
  - Identifiers (User ID) — used for app functionality and analytics

Data Not Linked to You:
  - Usage Data (Product Interaction) — used for analytics to improve the app
  - Diagnostics (Crash Data, Performance Data) — used for app stability monitoring

Data Not Collected:
  - Financial Info
  - Location (precise)
  - Browsing History
  - Contacts
  - Photos or Videos
  - Search History
  - Sensitive Info

Third-Party SDKs:
  - Supabase (authentication, database) — processes email and user data
  - Sentry (crash reporting) — processes anonymized crash/performance data
  - PostHog (analytics) — processes anonymized usage events
  - RevenueCat (subscriptions) — processes purchase/subscription data
